# Product

## Register

product

## Users

School and college students in India who take STEM courses (Robotics, AI, Electronics, IoT, Embedded Systems, Arduino, Drone Tech, Industry 4.0), plus their parents who pay for these courses.

## Product Purpose

MakersFlow is a STEM EdTech Learning Management System (LMS) designed for school and college students in India.

## Brand Personality

Clean, modern, and trustworthy for an education/payment product — not playful, not corporate-cold. Brand colors are indigo (#4F46E5) primary and orange (#FF6B35) accent.

## Anti-references

- Playful/childish EdTech styles (too informal)
- Corporate-cold, overly clinical platforms (lacking student engagement and modern EdTech appeal)

## Design Principles

- Trustworthy & Secure: Since parents are paying, the interface must feel secure, clear, and professional.
- Student-Centric & Modern: Keep learning surfaces engaging, clean, and interactive for students.
- High Information Clarity: Technical concepts like Robotics and AI require clear font choices, strong hierarchy, and breathable layouts.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Clear contrast matching WCAG level (especially since brand has indigo primary and orange accent).
- Responsive layouts suitable for varying screen sizes across devices in India.
